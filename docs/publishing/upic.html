ABOUT THIS PROJECT
I worked at the ZKM’s Hertz-Labor from October 2019 until February 2020. While there, I worked on this publication about the UPIC — a computerized compositional tool and musical instrument. I was responsible for handling the communication between the ZKM and the authors and assisted with the editing and layout. I proofread all the content and kept an eye out for all the small details. These included such things as picking out and editing images, making sure all the notation and references were consistent throughout, and generally verifying that the pagination was correct.
